PREAMBLE

This Business Investment Agreement is made and entered into between Noble Ink Ventures (hereinafter referred to as the "First Party") and the Second Party mentioned above.

The Second Party wishes to invest in the business of Noble Ink Ventures. The investment will be made through Bank Cheque / Online Transfer. The said cheque is being provided as Capital Security.

This agreement outlines the terms of investment, profit sharing, capital protection, and refund policy for the mutual benefit of both parties.

1. Nature of Business

Noble Ink Ventures operates a profitable investment and business venture. The Second Party agrees to invest in this business under the terms and conditions defined in this agreement.

2. Investment Amount

The Second Party agrees to invest a total sum of Pakistani Rupees Thirty Thousand (PKR 30,000) in the business of Noble Ink Ventures.

3. Mode of Investment & Capital Security

The investment amount of PKR 30,000 shall be paid via Bank Cheque / Online Transfer. The cheque is provided as Capital Security and shall be held safely by Noble Ink Ventures during the entire agreement period.

4. Duration

This agreement shall remain valid for a period of eight (8) months from the date of signing.

5. Profit Sharing

Noble Ink Ventures agrees to provide the Second Party with a monthly profit ranging between 4% to 6% monthly Returns against the capital Investment, depending on business performance.

6. Business Risk & Capital Protection

Both parties acknowledge that business performance may fluctuate. However, the Second Party’s original investment capital of PKR 100,000 shall remain fully safe and protected.

7. Refund Policy

Noble Ink Ventures guarantees the full refund of the invested capital (PKR 30,000) within 15 working days after the completion of the 8-month term. In case of early withdrawal, 30 days’ prior written notice is required and settlement will be done mutually.

8. Responsibilities of First Party (Noble Ink Ventures)

  • Manage and operate the business professionally and independently.
  • Ensure complete transparency in all operations.
  • Provide monthly profit payments on or before the 5th of each month.
  • Safeguard the invested capital at all times.

9. Responsibilities of Second Party

  • Provide the agreed investment amount of PKR 30,000.
  • Not interfere in the daily operations of the business.

10. Return of Capital

Noble Ink Ventures agrees to return the full invested capital of PKR 30,000 after successful completion of the eight (8) months term.

11. Mode of Payment

All payments (profit and capital refund) shall be made through bank transfer, online banking, or crossed cheque.
