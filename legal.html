Business Investment Agreement

Noble Ink Ventures — International Book Publishing

1. Nature of Business

The First Party operates an International Book Publishing Business. The Second Party agrees to invest in this business under the terms and conditions defined in this agreement.

2. Investment Amount

The Second Party agrees to invest a total sum of Pakistani Rupees Thirty Thousand (PKR 30,000) in the business of the First Party.

3. Duration

This agreement shall remain valid for a period of eight (8) months from the date of signing.

4. Profit Sharing

The First Party agrees to provide the Second Party with a monthly profit ranging between PKR 4,000 to PKR 5,000 per month, depending on business performance.

5. Business Risk & Capital Protection

Both parties acknowledge that business performance may fluctuate. Monthly profit may increase or decrease depending on business results. However, the Second Party's original investment capital of PKR 30,000 shall remain safe and protected and will be returned upon completion of the agreement period.

6. Responsibilities of First Party

  • Manage and operate the business independently.
  • Ensure transparency in operations.
  • Provide monthly profit payments.

7. Responsibilities of Second Party

  • Provide the agreed investment amount.
  • Shall not interfere in daily operations.

8. Return of Capital

The First Party agrees to return the full invested capital (PKR 30,000) after completion of eight (8) months.

9. Early Withdrawal

If the Second Party requests early withdrawal, at least 30 days' written notice must be provided. Settlement terms will be mutually agreed.

10. Mode of Payment

All payments shall be made through bank transfer, online banking, or cheque.
